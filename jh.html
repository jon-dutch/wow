Who, me?

Hi, I'm John! I've been working with MSF (Doctors Without Borders / Medecins Sans Frontieres) since 2015. First started in the field with the Ebola outbreak in Liberia as a Logistics Manager (aka "Log"). After a few field assignments I had the opportunity to work with MSF's Forced From Home traveling exhibition that moved throughout the US and gave the public a view into what life as a displaced person may look like.

At the start of the Covid-19 pandemic, I had the great honor to represent MSF whilst working with several Pueblo communities in the American Southwest. Our team provided technical and logistics support to various communities during a difficult time plagued with uncertainty and misinformation.

For the last few years I have been working with one of MSF's Learning & Development (L&D) units supporting our Ways of Working (WoW) management trainings.
